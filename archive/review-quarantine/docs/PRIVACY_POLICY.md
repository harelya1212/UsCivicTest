## Introduction

Civic Citizenship ("**we**", "**us**", "**our**", or "**Company**") operates the Civic Citizenship mobile application (the "**App**"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application across iOS, Android, and web platforms.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

#### Account Information
- **Name and Email** (optional): If you create an account, we collect your name and email for account management and communication.
- **Username and Password**: Securely stored for authentication.

#### Quiz & Interview Data
- **Quiz Responses**: Text answers you provide for civics questions
- **Audio Recordings**: Voice responses during interview mode
- **Follow-up Responses**: Answers to adaptive follow-up questions
- **Quiz Results**: Scores, grades (A/B/C/D), and performance metrics
- **Time Spent**: Duration on each question and overall session time
- **Progress Data**: Completion status, questions attempted, topics studied

#### User Preferences
- **Learning Preferences**: Quiz format preference, interview mode subscription
- **Notification Settings**: Your opt-in/out preferences
- **Accessibility Settings**: Text size, theme preferences, audio settings

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

#### Device Information
- **Device Type**: iPhone, iPad, Android phone/tablet
- **Device OS**: iOS version, Android API level
- **App Version**: Current version of Civic Citizenship installed
- **Device ID**: Unique identifier for analytics purposes only
- **Device Model**: For performance and compatibility tracking

#### Usage Analytics (Firebase Analytics)
- **App Opens**: When and how frequently you use the app
- **Screen Views**: Which screens/features you access
- **Action Events**: Buttons clicked, quizzes started, questions submitted
- **Session Duration**: How long you spend in the app
- **Crashes and Errors**: Technical issues to help improve stability

#### Audio Permission Logs (iOS/Android-level)
- **Microphone Access**: When microphone permission is requested/granted
- **Permission Status**: Current state of your permissions
- **Audio Recording Sessions**: When you record (timestamp, not content)

#### Advertising Data (AdMob)
- **Ad Impressions**: Ads shown to you
- **Ad Interactions**: Taps on ads
- **Ad Network Data**: Which ad networks serve your ads
- **Estimated Location**: Approximate geographic area for ad targeting (not precise GPS)

#### Crash Reporting
- **Crash Logs**: Stack traces when the app crashes
- **Session Info**: Context of the crash (screen, action)
- **Memory Usage**: Device memory state at time of crash

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### 3.1 Service Providers (Third Parties)

#### Google Firebase
- **What We Share**: Quiz responses, scores, user IDs
- **Purpose**: Analytics, data storage, crash reporting
- **Data Processing**: Google processes data per their DPA
- **More Info**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

#### Google AdMob Advertising
- **What We Share**: Device ID, device type, usage patterns, estimated location
- **Purpose**: Serve personalized ads
- **Data Processing**: Google's advertising policies apply
- **Ad Targeting**: Based on device and in-app behavior only (not personal data)
- **More Info**: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

#### Firebase Cloud Storage
- **What We Share**: Audio files (during recording sessions only)
- **Purpose**: Temporary storage during speech-to-text processing
- **Retention**: Audio deleted after speech-to-text transcription (typically < 5 minutes)
- **Security**: Encrypted in transit and at rest

### 3.2 What We Do NOT Share
- ❌ Personal name, email, or contact information with advertisers
- ❌ Raw quiz responses or answer content with third parties
- ❌ Audio files with advertisers or external services
- ❌ Complete user profiles or account credentials
- ❌ Information to data brokers or for sale

### 3.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, including:
- Court orders or legal subpoena
- Law enforcement requests (with proper warrants)
- National security matters
- To protect our rights or safety
- To prevent fraud or criminal activity

## 4. Data Retention

### Quiz & Performance Data
- **Retention Period**: 180 days (6 months) after last app activity
- **Automatic Deletion**: Deleted when you choose "Clear My Data" in settings
- **Manual Deletion**: Use the "Delete Account" option in settings for complete removal

### Audio Recordings
- **Retention Period**: NOT PERMANENTLY STORED
  - Live processing only (converted to text via speech-to-text)
  - Deleted from temporary storage within 5 minutes
  - Only transcript stored (if enabled by user)
- **Emergency Access**: Deleted even in crash recovery

### Firebase Analytics
- **Retention Period**: 14 months rolling (auto-deleted automatically by Google)
- **Aggregated Only**: Individual identifiers removed after 30 days
- **User-Level Deletion**: Requesting data deletion removes you from analytics

### AdMob & Advertising Data
- **Retention Period**: 30 days
- **Interest Profiles**: Updated monthly
- **Cookie Equivalent**: Reset when you sign out

### Account Data
- **Active Account**: Retained as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Account**: Removed within 30 days of deletion request
- **Backup Copies**: May be retained for 90 days for disaster recovery

### 7.1 Security Measures We Use
- **Encryption in Transit**: HTTPS/SSL for all data transmission
- **Encryption at Rest**: AES-256 encryption for stored data
- **Firebase Security**: Google's industry-standard security infrastructure
- **Access Controls**: Only necessary team members can access data
- **Regular Audits**: Monthly security reviews

### 7.2 What We Don't Do
- ❌ Store passwords in plain text
- ❌ Store audio files permanently
- ❌ Transmit data over unencrypted connections
- ❌ Share encryption keys
- ❌ Log sensitive user information

### 7.3 Limitations
While we use industry-standard security, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

## 14. Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Is my location tracked?
**A:** No. We do not use GPS or request location permissions. AdMob may estimate your region for ad targeting, but not precise location.

### Q: Are my audio recordings saved?
**A:** No. Audio is briefly stored during speech-to-text processing (~5 minutes) then permanently deleted. Only the transcript may be saved if enabled.

### Q: Can I see what data you have about me?
**A:** Yes. Email support@civiceducation.example.com with "Data Access Request" and we'll provide it within 30 days.

### Q: How do I delete my account?
**A:** In-App: Settings → Account → Delete Account. All associated data will be deleted within 30 days.

### Q: Do you sell my data?
**A:** No. We never sell personal data. We only share anonymized analytics with Google Firebase for service improvement.

### Q: Can I stop seeing ads?
**A:** Ads are part of the free app model. Future versions may offer ad-free paid option.

### Q: What about children using the app?
**A:** If under 13, require parental consent. We use reduced data collection for minors (no behavioral targeting).

### Q: Who can I contact with privacy questions?
**A:** support@civiceducation.example.com
