- ✅ **Large 4-Answer Multiple Choice** - 70px minimum height buttons for easy tapping
- ✅ **Visual Color Feedback** - Green (✓) for correct, Red (✗) for wrong
- ✅ **User-Controlled Pacing** - NO auto-advance, user taps to continue
- ✅ **Official Answers Always Visible** - Prominently displayed in green box
- ✅ **Visual Explanations** - On-demand educational content with emoji context
- ✅ **Adaptive Difficulty** - Easy → Hard based on 80%+ or <50% performance

- ✅ **Performance Tracking** - Last 10 answers determine difficulty
